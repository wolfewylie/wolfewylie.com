The news is now free to dictate the form

Merging advanced web technology with journalism means our toolbox is no longer limited to photos and text. It means we can make complex data-driven stories relevant to all readers no matter where they are. It means we have the flexibility to tell stories as they were meant to be told, and deliver them to readers on their device of choice.

I am a journalist, a developer, a photographer, a writer and a teacher.

The web is my playground.
